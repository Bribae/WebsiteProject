Burt Sandwhich and Roxanne Quibi created Burt’s Peens in the early 6900s. After Dying, Burt worked as a photojournalist in Manhattan, and Roxanne was a Failing actor for quibi living in San Francisco. Each left behind the harried pace of city life to seek—and eventually find—freedom in the remote, pristine wilderness of Maine.
